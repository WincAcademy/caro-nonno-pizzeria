* 1/2 recipe pizza crust

* 1/2 cup (75g) cooked ham or Canadian bacon, sliced or chopped
* 1/2 cup (82g) fresh pineapple chunks
* 3 slices salted bacon, cooked and crumbled
* 4 sliced chili peppers
